10 Surah (Roman Urdu)

1. Surah Al-Fatiha

Bismillah hir Rahman nir Raheem. Alhamdu lillaahi Rabbil 'aalameen. Ar-Rahmaanir-Raheem. Maaliki Yawmid-Deen. Iyyaaka na'budu wa lyyaaka nasta'een. Ihdinas-Siraatal-Mustaqeem. Siraatal-lazeena an'amta 'alaihim ghayril-maghdoobi 'alaihim wa lad-daaalleen.

2. Surah Al-Ikhlas

Bismillah hir Rahman nir Raheem. Qul huwal laahu ahad. Allah hus-Samad. Lam yalid wa lam yoolad. Wa lam yakul-lahu kufuwan ahad.

3. Surah Al-Falaq

Bismillah hir Rahman nir Raheem. Qul a'oozu bi rabbil-falaq. Min sharri maa khalaq. Wa min sharri ghaasiqin iza waqab. Wa min sharrin-naffaa-saati fil 'uqad. Wa min sharri haasidin iza hasad.

4. Surah An-Naas

Bismillah hir Rahman nir Raheem. Qul a'oozu bi rabbin naas. Malikin naas. Ilaahin naas. Min sharril waswaasil khannaas. Allazee yuwaswisu fee sudoorin naas. Minal jinnati wan naas.

5. Surah Al-Kawthar

Bismillah hir Rahman nir Raheem. Inna a'tainaa kal kawthar. Fasalli li rabbika wanhar. Inna shaani'aka huwal abtar.

6. Surah Al-Kafirun

Bismillah hir Rahman nir Raheem. Qul yaa ayyuhal kaafiroon. Laa a'budu ma t'abudoon. Wa laa antum 'abidoona maa a'bud. Wa laa ana 'abidum maa 'abattum. Wa laa antum 'abidoona maa a'bud. Lakum deenukum wa liya deen.

7. Surah Al-Ma'un

Ara'aytal lazee yukazzibu bid-deen. Fa zaalikal lazee yadu'ul-yateem. Wa laa yahuddu 'alaa ta'aamil miskeen. Fa wailul-lil musalleen. Allazeena hum 'an salaatihim saahoon. Allazeena hum yuraaa'oon. Wa yamna'oonal ma'oon.

8. Surah Quraysh

Li-ee-laafi quraish. Ee-laafihim rihlatash shitaa'i was-saif. Fal-ya'budoo rabba haazal-bait. Allazeee at'amahum min joo'inw-wa-aamanahum min khawf.

9. Surah Al-Masad

Tabbat yadaa abee Lahabinw-wa tabb. Maa aghnaa 'anhu maaluhu wa maa kasab. Sa-yaslaa naaran zaata lahab. Wamra-atuhu hamma latal-hatab. Fee jeediha hablum mim-masad.

10. Surah Al-Asr

Wal 'asr. Innal insaana lafee khusr. Illal lazeena aamanoo wa 'amilus saalihaati wa tawaasaw bil haqqi wa tawaasaw bis sabr.
